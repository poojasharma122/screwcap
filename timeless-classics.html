Timeless Classics

These are the stays that have stood the test of time. The Timeless Classics collection features historic inns, beautifully restored estates, and boutique lodges across New Zealand — each with a story to tell, a strong sense of place, and the kind of atmosphere that doesn’t need trends to make an impression.

Featured Heritage & Character Stays

From century-old villas to lakeside lodges with history in their bones, these properties offer more than just a room. They deliver experience, craftsmanship, and a setting that feels rooted — perfect for travellers who prefer charm over flash.

Eichardt’s Private Hotel – Queenstown

Price Per Night: $1050 NZD

  • Historic lakefront hotel with boutique luxury
  • Built in 1860s, now fully restored and reimagined
  • Elegant interiors, fireplaces, and lake-facing suites

Eichardt’s is Queenstown’s original hotel — and still one of its best. With heritage detailing, award-winning service, and lake views that haven’t changed in a century, this stay is equal parts atmosphere and polish.

Book Now

The Duke of Marlborough – Russell, Bay of Islands

Price Per Night: $330 NZD

  • New Zealand’s first licensed hotel, est. 1827
  • Waterfront location with balcony rooms
  • Pub, dining, and history all under one roof

The Duke blends casual charm with historic presence. Sitting right on the Bay of Islands waterfront, it’s been serving travellers for generations — now with modern rooms, fresh seafood, and wine by the sea.

Book Now

Fable Dunedin – Dunedin

Price Per Night: $410 NZD

  • Heritage-listed building with modern luxury interiors
  • Located in Dunedin’s historic quarter
  • In-room cocktail service and tailored menus

Fable Dunedin is a boutique hotel that balances old and new beautifully. The exterior holds strong to its roots, while the inside delivers high-thread comfort and quiet, moody elegance — perfect for guests who want character with consistency.

Book Now

Riverstone House – Martinborough

Price Per Night: $325 NZD

  • Restored villa in the heart of wine country
  • Classic design with garden views and open-air living
  • Walking distance to wineries and cafes

This quiet retreat channels early 1900s charm without losing any modern-day comfort. It’s a great base for exploring Martinborough’s cellar doors by day, and unwinding on the verandah with a glass of Syrah at sunset.

Book Now

The Grand by SkyCity – Auckland

Price Per Night: $310 NZD

  • Blends modern luxury with nods to Auckland’s past
  • Historic references in design and curated artwork
  • Access to dining, casino, and cultural venues

Though modern at first glance, The Grand weaves in references to Auckland’s cultural and architectural heritage throughout its design. Ideal for travellers who want city convenience with more texture than a standard glass tower.

Book Now

Martinborough Hotel – Martinborough

Price Per Night: $295 NZD

  • 1882-built hotel with wraparound balconies
  • Rooms named after local winemakers and poets
  • Steps from restaurants, vineyards, and tasting rooms

The Martinborough Hotel delivers timeless village atmosphere. It’s full of original timber, regional personality, and long-form storytelling — from its veranda to its old guest books.

Book Now

Warwick House – Nelson

Price Per Night: $365 NZD

  • One of New Zealand’s oldest Gothic Revival homes
  • Antique furnishings, chandeliers, and turrets
  • Personalised hosted experience

This historic residence offers a boutique stay full of dramatic detail. With turret rooms, elegant high ceilings, and traditional service, Warwick House is ideal for guests who want a slower, story-filled stay.

Book Now

Otahuna Lodge – Tai Tapu, Canterbury

Price Per Night: $2250 NZD

  • 1895 mansion estate with formal gardens
  • Seven exclusive suites, each individually styled
  • Degustation dining, curated wine cellar, and historic tours

Otahuna is history preserved — and elevated. This property is a world-class lodge that hasn’t forgotten its roots. With fireplaces, antique decor, and a grounds-to-table philosophy, it delivers heritage at its highest level.
