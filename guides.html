Screwcap Guides

New Zealand is an exciting travel destination offering a mix of world-class wine, natural wellness, and thrilling adventures. The Screwcap Guides introduce you to the best ways to experience it all.

Vineyards:

New Zealand's wine regions, from Northland to Central Otago, each boast a unique flavor and landscape. Our Vineyards page provides insights into key wine-growing areas, varietals, and planning your visit – be it a cellar door tasting in Marlborough, a long lunch in Hawke’s Bay, or exploring a hidden boutique winery in the South Island. Find tips on wine tours, accommodations, and the best seasons to visit.

Spas:

Wellness travel in New Zealand blends luxury with nature. The Spas page features coastal retreats, alpine day spas, and geothermal experiences. Find information on regional offerings – from volcanic mud baths to forest-surrounded thermal pools. We highlight packages combining massage, facials, hot springs, and fine dining, plus options for couples, solo travelers, and those seeking a full escape.

Adventure Resorts:

For travelers who crave excitement, the Adventure Resorts page covers places where luxury meets adrenaline. Discover mountain lodges with heli-ski access, lakeside resorts near world-class mountain biking trails, and properties partnered with skydiving or jet boating operators. The guide details the types of experiences available in different regions and how to combine them with high-end accommodation, great food, and stunning scenery.
