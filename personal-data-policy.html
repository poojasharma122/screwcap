Personal Data Policy

At Screwcap, we are committed to protecting your personal data and handling it with care, transparency, and purpose. This Personal Data Policy explains what types of personal data we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and your rights under New Zealand law.

This policy is part of our broader Privacy Policy and applies to all personal information submitted via our website (www.screwcap.co.nz), forms, or email communications.

1. What Is Personal Data?

Personal data refers to any information that identifies you as an individual. This may include:

  • Your name

  • Email address

  • Region or location

  • Communication history with us

  • Website interaction data (e.g., IP address, device type, usage behaviour)

  • Any additional details you provide through forms or contact channels

We do not collect financial details or sensitive personal information.

2. How We Collect Personal Data

We collect personal data through the following channels:

  • When you contact us through the Screwcap website

  • When you subscribe to our newsletter or travel updates

  • When you submit an accommodation listing or enquiry

  • When you engage with us by email

  • Through analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) that track site usage

All data collection is lawful, limited to what is necessary, and processed for clear, stated purposes.

3. How We Use Personal Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Respond to enquiries and provide customer support

  • Send newsletters or updates you’ve opted into

  • Assess property listing submissions or partnership requests

  • Improve website functionality and user experience

  • Analyse site traffic and user preferences via anonymised analytics

We do not use your data for profiling or automated decision-making.

4. Lawful Basis for Processing

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, we only collect and process personal data where:

  • You have given explicit consent

  • It is necessary for communication or providing a requested service

  • We are legally required to retain or disclose the information

  • It is reasonably required for operational purposes, such as managing our website

5. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected. When no longer needed, the data is securely deleted or anonymised unless we are legally required to retain it longer.

6. Storage and Security

All personal data is stored securely using trusted hosting providers and standard encryption practices. Access is limited to authorised personnel. We take reasonable steps to prevent loss, unauthorised access, misuse, or disclosure.

7. Sharing of Personal Data

Screwcap does not sell or rent personal data. We may share limited information with:

  • Service providers (e.g., email marketing platforms, analytics tools) for site functionality

  • Regulatory or legal authorities where required by law

  • Business partners in the case of a listing, with your permission

All third parties must comply with strict privacy and data handling standards.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you

  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information

  • Request deletion of your data, where applicable

  • Withdraw consent to communication at any time

  • Complain to the New Zealand Privacy Commissioner if you believe your rights have been violated

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us directly at info@screwcap.co.nz.

9. International Visitors

Screwcap operates in New Zealand but may be accessed globally. By using the site from outside New Zealand, you acknowledge that your data will be processed and stored in accordance with New Zealand law, which may differ from your local data protection regulations.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Personal Data Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted here with a new effective date. Continued use of our site after updates implies agreement with the revised terms.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about how your personal data is collected or handled, or you’d like to make a request regarding your data:

Email: info@screwcap.co.nz
