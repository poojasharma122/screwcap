Privacy Policy

At Screwcap, we take privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or use any of our services. We are committed to complying with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 and ensuring that your data is handled responsibly and transparently.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and store the following types of personal information:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact details: Name, email address, and any information you provide via our contact forms or newsletter sign-up.

  • Usage data: Information about how you use the Screwcap website (e.g., pages viewed, time spent, clicks, IP address, and browser type).

  • Communication records: Any messages, feedback, or email correspondence you send us.

  • Listing enquiries: Information submitted when suggesting accommodation or applying for partnership opportunities.

We do not collect payment details, credit card numbers, or sensitive personal identifiers through this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer support

  • Send you updates, newsletters, or promotional material (only if you’ve opted in)

  • Improve our website functionality and user experience

  • Analyse trends and website performance through analytics tools

  • Evaluate and manage property listing applications or partnerships

You can unsubscribe from any promotional communication at any time using the link in the footer of our emails or by contacting us directly.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may share your data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the website (e.g., email platforms, analytics tools, hosting services), under the condition that they comply with applicable privacy laws and only use the information for agreed purposes.

We may also disclose your information if legally required to do so by court order, law enforcement, or government authority.

4. Data Storage and Security

All personal information is stored securely using industry-standard safeguards. We implement reasonable technical, administrative, and physical measures to protect your data against loss, misuse, and unauthorised access.

Despite our best efforts, no digital platform can guarantee complete security. By using Screwcap, you acknowledge this and accept that you share data at your own risk.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Screwcap uses cookies and third-party tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to enhance your browsing experience and collect usage data. These tools help us understand how visitors use our site so we can make improvements.

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may limit the functionality of certain pages.

6. Your Rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you

  • Request correction of any inaccuracies in your information

  • Withdraw consent to marketing communications at any time

  • Request deletion of your personal information, where applicable

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@screwcap.co.nz.

7. Third-Party Links

Screwcap may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before sharing personal information.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a child has submitted information through our site, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page with a new effective date. We recommend checking this page periodically to stay informed.

10. Contact Us
