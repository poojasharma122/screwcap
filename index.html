Curated Hotel Stays Across New Zealand

Screwcap is a handpicked collection of places to stay around the country — from vineyard lodges to lakeside retreats, boutique inns, and adventure basecamps. Every property is selected for its setting, character, and connection to the land.

From Marlborough’s open skies to the stillness of Lake Tarawera, Screwcap brings together accommodation that balances comfort with experience. We focus on places that feel right — not just for a night’s sleep, but for what the stay adds to the trip itself. Some come with views over vines, others with trails at the doorstep. All offer something that stays with you.

Why We Built Screwcap

Screwcap is about getting rid of the clutter. No endless scrolls. No generic lists. Just places that are worth your time. We name the site after the shift that happened in New Zealand winemaking — a move toward something practical, reliable, and refreshingly better.

We’ve applied the same idea to how we approach travel.

What Makes a Screwcap Stay?

  • Each One Is Handpicked

We look for properties with quality, location, and character — and skip the rest.

  • Location Is the Priority

Our listings reflect the best of each region, from alpine views to coastline quiet.

  • Details That Matter

You’ll find notes on regional food, nearby cellar doors, or standout design touches — the kind of extras that round out a stay.
