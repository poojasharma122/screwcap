Cellar Door Collection

Stay steps away from the pour. The Cellar Door Collection brings together New Zealand accommodation with unmatched access to some of the country’s best wineries. These properties are all within walking distance of a working cellar door — so your next tasting is never far.

Featured Cellar Door Stays

From vineyard lodges to countryside cottages, each stay offers direct or immediate access to a cellar door. These listings are for travellers who want their time in wine country to start right at the door — no driving, no delays.

The Kinross Cottages – Gibbston Valley, Central Otago

Price Per Night: $350 NZD

  • Multiple boutique labels available on site
  • Daily tastings and wine education experiences
  • Surrounded by mountains and Pinot vines

Kinross is more than a stay — it’s a wine village of its own. Guests can taste from several top-tier Central Otago producers at the cellar door just metres from their cottage. It’s relaxed, informative, and beautifully located in the valley between Queenstown and Cromwell.

The Farm at Cape Kidnappers – Hawke’s Bay

Price Per Night: $1650 NZD

  • Onsite cellar selection and regional wine focus
  • Luxury lodge with sweeping coastal vineyard views
  • Wine pairing dinners and curated tastings

Cape Kidnappers is known for its dramatic location, but its cellar is just as impressive. With deep ties to Hawke’s Bay’s winemaking community, the lodge offers cellar door-style tasting experiences and access to rare regional wines — all without leaving the property.

More Info

Herzog Vineyard Cottage – Marlborough

Price Per Night: $420 NZD

  • Private stay on a respected organic vineyard
  • Walk to the family-run cellar door and restaurant
  • Peaceful garden setting among the vines

This one-bedroom cottage is situated beside the vines of Hans Herzog’s estate. Guests can explore the wines at the cellar door next door, then return to their private space with a bottle (or two). An intimate stay designed for slow sips and quiet evenings.

More Info

Black Estate B&B – Waipara, North Canterbury

Price Per Night: $360 NZD

  • Onsite tasting room and award-winning winery
  • Minimalist rooms overlooking vines and hills
  • Organic produce and natural wine focus

This North Canterbury spot is effortlessly stylish and grounded. The rooms sit behind the cellar door, overlooking the valley and vineyard. It’s a great match for travellers looking for something pared back, natural, and tuned in to seasonal rhythms.

More Info

Te Whare Ra Vineyard Loft – Marlborough

Price Per Night: $310 NZD

  • Above the Te Whare Ra tasting room
  • Boutique, biodynamic wine producer
  • Close to other key cellar doors in the region

Stay directly above one of Marlborough’s most respected small producers. The loft offers a light-filled space with vineyard views and direct access to the winery below. Perfect for wine enthusiasts seeking something off the radar but deeply authentic.

More Info

The Man O’ War Cottages – Waiheke Island

Price Per Night: $395 NZD

  • Steps from the cellar door on the island’s eastern edge
  • Rustic beachfront charm surrounded by vines
  • Secluded, scenic, and ideal for weekend wine escapes

Man O’ War is one of Waiheke’s most iconic wine names — and its cottages let you stay within walking distance of the tasting room and beach. It’s a rare combination of coast, vines, and comfortable lodging in a truly unspoiled part of the island.

More Info

Carrick Lodge – Bannockburn, Central Otago

Price Per Night: $335 NZD

  • Small, stylish lodge near Carrick Winery
  • Vineyard dining and cellar tastings nearby
  • Great access to biking trails and other wineries

Set along the Bannockburn wine trail, Carrick Lodge offers clean, comfortable stays just down the road from one of Central Otago’s best cellar doors. It’s an ideal base for those exploring the region on foot or by bike.

More Info

Riverby Homestead – Marlborough

Price Per Night: $385 NZD

  • Private guesthouse on a working vineyard
  • Walkable to Riverby Wines tasting room
  • Surrounded by vines and rural calm

This restored farmhouse offers a spacious, peaceful retreat next to one of Marlborough’s family-owned wineries. With no neighbours and plenty of outdoor space, it’s perfect for couples looking to immerse themselves in wine country without distractions.
