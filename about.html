About

Screwcap is a curated travel and accommodation platform focused on helping people experience New Zealand through places worth staying in. We feature vineyard retreats, spa lodges, adventure bases, cellar-door hideaways, casino resorts, and timeless hotels — all chosen for their setting, substance, and connection to the landscape.

The name Screwcap pays homage to one of the most quietly disruptive innovations in New Zealand’s wine industry. Just as the screwcap replaced tradition with clarity, we aim to do the same in travel — cutting through noise, ignoring overused luxury clichés, and spotlighting places that actually offer something memorable.

What We Do

We don’t try to list everything. Instead, we focus on accommodation that:

  • Reflects its location in a real and tangible way

  • Offers something distinctive — in setting, style, or story

  • Is consistently reviewed as comfortable, professional, and well-run

  • Connects naturally to food, wine, wellness, or outdoor experience

Whether it’s a lakefront villa in Central Otago, a vineyard cottage in Marlborough, a heritage inn in Russell, or a casino hotel in Auckland, every listing on Screwcap has been individually reviewed, selected, and written for travellers who want more than just a place to sleep.

Why We’re Different

Screwcap exists because modern travel content has become crowded and generic. Our focus is on curated stays, transparent storytelling, and connecting people to real, grounded travel experiences.

We don’t use vague rankings, clickbait headlines, or pay-to-list shortcuts. If a hotel is on Screwcap, it’s there because it offers something that fits — something with texture, with purpose, and with that sense of “this was the right place to book.”

Our Approach to Curation

We work with a mix of independent research, regional contacts, verified guest insights, and local tourism resources. Listings are not automated or AI-generated. Each page is reviewed for accuracy, tone, and originality. If a property no longer meets the standard, it’s removed.

We're also intentional about language. You won’t find filler phrases or recycled descriptions here — just clear, honest overviews that reflect the quality of the stay.

Our Audience

Screwcap is designed for travellers who care where they stay. That might mean someone booking a vineyard lodge for a slow weekend in Waipara, a couple planning a spa retreat in Rotorua, or a group heading to Queenstown for a casino break with scenery on the side.

Most of our audience is based in New Zealand or travelling here from Australia, North America, or Europe. They’re often wine lovers, outdoor enthusiasts, or design-conscious guests looking for hotels that don’t feel manufactured.

Looking Ahead

Screwcap is constantly evolving. We’re adding new listings region by region, publishing articles through the Screwcap Journal, and refining our categories to reflect how people actually travel. We’ll always prioritise thoughtful curation over quantity — and keep the wine-country attitude running quietly in the background.
